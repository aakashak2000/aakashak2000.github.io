About

I’m Aakash Kumar, currently pursuing my MS in Computer Science (AI) at USC Viterbi. Before coming here, I spent about 4.5 years building production AI systems across LLMs, RAG, document intelligence, multimodal AI, and applied machine learning for real enterprise use cases.

Over time, while working on these systems in the real world, I realised I didn’t just enjoy building things that work. I also wanted to go deeper into why they work, where they fail, and what makes them hold up under real constraints. That was a big part of why I chose to do my master’s after working for a few years.

Before USC, I worked at Arya.ai, where I grew from Data Scientist to Senior Research Scientist and worked on AI systems for banks, insurers, and enterprise clients. Right now, I’m especially interested in the intersection of machine learning theory and deployed systems, understanding models more fundamentally, and then building them in ways that are actually reliable and useful.

At USC, I’m also conducting research in medical imaging, multimodal learning, and synthetic data generation.
