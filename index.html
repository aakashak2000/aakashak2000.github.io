About

I'm an MS CS student (AI track) at USC Viterbi, building on nearly five years of industry experience shipping production AI systems for financial institutions across India and the Middle East.

Before USC, I spent 4.5 years at Arya.ai — an AI-FinTech startup — progressing from Data Scientist to Senior Research Scientist. I led development of production RAG pipelines, document intelligence systems (OCR/IDP), multimodal AI, and LLM-powered financial tools that serve real clients at scale.

My interests sit at the intersection of ML theory and deployed systems — understanding why models work and building things that actually run in production. I'm currently deepening my theoretical foundations at USC while staying close to applied work through personal projects and writing.

Targeting SWE AI/ML, AI Engineer, or ML Engineer roles at FAANG, frontier AI labs, or well-funded startups. Open to summer 2026 opportunities.
